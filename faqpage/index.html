FAQ

How do I register for Essence'21 ?

Registration can be done on - https://essencefest.netlify.app/registerlogin by filling in your personal details. Then you can register for any event by clicking on that particular event and following there.

What should I do after completing registration?

After registering, you will get an email to verify your registration. Keep following our updates on social media and email.

Is the entry free for all ? Who can attend ?

Entry to Essence will be absolutely free. Only college students (and those who have passed the 12th but havent been alloted a college yet) are allowed

Can I cancel my goodie order?

No, If you buy a goodie once you can't cancel your order.

Can I unregister myself from events?

Yes, you can unregister yourself from any event you registered too.

How can I be a part of the organizing team of Essence ?

You can become a college ambassador for your college, and you will become the primary point of contact between your college and the Aurora'20 team. For further queries you may reach out to us here
